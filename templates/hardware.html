Full hardware setup

This is the entire setup I am using for measuring temperature and humidity in my home office. Below you will see each individual part and it's price, in case you want to replicate this project or just curious.

Total price: 187 SEK

Raspberry Pi Pico WH 2022

This is a microcontroller board which enables measurment of the physical world, in this case temperature and humidity.

Price: 109 SEK

DHT11

This is the sensor which connects to the microcontroller through the breadboard and collects and sends data.

Price: 49 SEK

Cables

These are the cables which are used in order to transfer electricity to the sensor, as well as transfering the measured data to the microcontroller.

Price: 29 SEK
